**hook_patterns:** The hook interrupts a mundane action with an unexpected discovery, creating instant intrigue before the viewer knows what was found. The word "shocked" raises stakes preemptively, holding attention through the reveal.

**pacing:** The script moves through a nurturing arc with deliberate slowness — discovery → decision → daily care → growth → emotional resolution. The pacing mirrors real time passage, which earns the emotional weight of the final line.

**why_it_works:** The audience is primed to feel protective over orphaned animals, and the writer leans into that instinct from sentence two. The final line reframes the entire story — from rescue to belonging — in a single image, delivering both emotional resolution and a memorable closing beat.
